## 📊 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard helps stakeholders understand the sales and profitability performance of a fictional US-based superstore. The analysis covers KPIs, product performance, regional trends, discount impact, and customer segments.

**Total Records (Cleaned):** 9,977 (17 duplicate rows removed)

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **KPI Cards**: Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Quantity, Profit Margin
- **Top 10 Products** by Sales (Bar Chart)
- **Sales by Region** (Horizontal Bar)
- **Sales Distribution by Category** (Donut Chart)
- **Profit Trend by Discount** (Line Chart)
- **Loss-Making Sub-Categories** Analysis
- **Sales & Profit by Customer Segment** (Stacked Bar)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Technology category contributes the highest share (~36.41%) in sales.
- West and East regions are the top performers.
- High discounts significantly reduce profit and often lead to losses.
- **Tables** is the biggest loss-making sub-category.
- Consumer segment drives maximum sales and profit.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

1. Optimize discount policy — Avoid high discounts (>30%) on low-margin products like Tables and Bookcases.
2. Re-evaluate pricing and supplier costs for loss-making sub-categories.
3. Focus marketing efforts on Central and South regions.
4. Continue promoting Technology products (Phones, Accessories, Copiers).
5. Explore upselling strategies in Corporate and Home Office segments.
